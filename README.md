# Retail Sales Performance & Business Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to evaluate revenue performance, customer behavior, and regional sales distribution. The dashboard provides multi-dimensional insights into business growth and operational efficiency.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Track total sales and order trends over time
- Analyze regional performance
- Identify top-performing categories
- Evaluate average sales per order
- Understand customer distribution

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Sales per Order
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Regional Contribution
- Category-wise Sales

## 📈 Key Insights
- Identified peak sales months and seasonal patterns
- Highlighted highest revenue-generating regions
- Determined top-performing product categories
- Observed order frequency and customer trends

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
